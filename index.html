I am a second‑year Master’s student in Robotics at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Zac Manchester. My research focuses on simulation, learning, and control algorithms for contact-rich and high-dimensional robotic systems. I am currently applying for Ph.D. programs in Mechanical Engineering, Robotics, and Computer Science.

Previously, I received my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Georgia Tech in 2022, where I was advised by Ye Zhao and Matthew Gombolay. My undergraduate research focused on trajectory optmization and multi-agent reinforcement learning.
